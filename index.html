About the clinic

Care that takes its time to explain itself.

Patients keep telling us the same thing: they felt informed, not rushed. At The Smile Clinique, every procedure — from a routine cleaning to a root canal — is walked through before it begins, in plain language, with honest, fair pricing.

The clinic is kept spotless and quiet, the team is unfailingly patient, and care is taken to make even nervous first-time patients and children feel at ease in the chair.

  • Hygiene-first, sterilised equipment for every patient
  • Transparent costs — agreed before any work begins
  • A calm, kid-friendly approach to dental anxiety
